# Security Incident Ticket: INC-2023-0915-EMOTET-042

## Summary
Emotet banking trojan identified through behavioral detection on corporate laptop. Malware was delivered via a weaponized Excel spreadsheet disguised as a shipping invoice, executing through rundll32.exe loader mechanism.

## Attack Chain

1. **Initial Access**: Phishing email with fake DHL shipping notification
2. **User Action**: Victim opened malicious Excel attachment
3. **Macro Execution**: VBA macro dropped intermediate loader
4. **Payload Delivery**: Loader invoked shipping_notice_092023.dll via rundll32
5. **Detection**: Falcon detected known Emotet behavioral signature
6. **Containment**: Endpoint quarantined, process terminated

## Related Intelligence

- **Campaign**: Emotet September 2023 distribution wave

- **TTPs**: Excel 4.0 macros, DLL side-loading, rundll32 abuse

## Analyst Notes

This incident is part of the ongoing Emotet resurgence observed since early 2023. The threat actor used shipping notification lures, which is a common social engineering tactic for Emotet campaigns. The behavioral detection successfully identified the rundll32-based execution pattern characteristic of Emotet loaders.

Network forensics revealed no successful C2 communication prior to containment. The rapid detection and response prevented credential theft and potential lateral movement.
